How Manual Feedback Works

Since you're using the free version of Web3Forms, automated email replies to applicants are not available. Here's how to send feedback manually:

  1. When someone submits a form, you receive it in YOUR email inbox
  2. The submission is also saved in this admin panel
  3. To send feedback, click the "Send Feedback" button next to any application
  4. This opens your default email client with a pre-filled message
  5. Review the message, add any custom notes, and send
  6. The system will track that feedback was sent
